[breencast_jumbotron_standard.vmf]

Description: This is the smaller of the two jumbotron monitors. This one is used more commonly than the big one. The maps it is seen in are background01.bsp, d1_trainstation_01.bsp, d1_trainstation_02.bsp, d3_c17_03.bsp, ep1_background02.bsp and ep1_c17_01.bsp.

Configuration: Select all 6 func_monitor entities and change the 'Camera name' parameter to 'camera_tv_jumboslate'. Select the env_microphone entity and change the 'Measure target' to 'target_breen_micsamplepoint' for Breencasts or 'info_kleinermic_samplepoint' for Kleinercasts.
