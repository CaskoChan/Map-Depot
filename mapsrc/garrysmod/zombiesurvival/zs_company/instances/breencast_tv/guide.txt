[breencast_tv.vmf]

Description: This is the small TV seen inside indoor settings. The maps that this is featured in are d1_trainstation_03.bsp, d3_c17_04.bsp and ep1_c17_02b.bsp.

Configuration: Select the func_monitor entity and change the 'Camera name' parameter to 'camera_tv_tvslate'. Select the env_microphone entity and change the 'Measure target' to 'target_breen_micsamplepoint' for Breencasts or 'info_kleinermic_samplepoint' for Kleinercasts.

Extra info: The cord connecting it to the wall can snap if you take it too far away from the wall. You can also break the tv by hitting it with the crowbar or shooting it.
