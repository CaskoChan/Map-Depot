[breencast_jumbotron_big.vmf]

Description: This is the bigger of the two jumbotron monitors. This one is only seen in d1_trainstation_01.bsp and d3_citadel_04.bsp.

Configuration: Select all 6 func_monitor entities and change the 'Camera name' parameter to 'camera_tv_jumboslate'. Select the env_microphone entity and change the 'Measure target' to 'target_breen_micsamplepoint' for Breencasts or 'info_kleinermic_samplepoint' for Kleinercasts.
